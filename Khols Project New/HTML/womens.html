Women's Jeans On Sale

One type of apparel item that is essential for any woman’s wardrobe is jeans. And when you’re searching for denim, shop our full line of on-sale women’s jeans. We offer plenty of styles and fits that are sure to suit your personal style, so you can feel confident that you’ll find a pair that works for you! Here’s a look at the variety of women’s jeans styles you can choose from:

  • Bootcut Jeans - Bootcut jeans are slim-fitting through the thighs and knees. They feature a slightly flared opening that can be anywhere from 16 inches to 22 inches in diameter. The wider opening makes them ideal for layering over boots and allows the jeans themselves to be perfectly paired with a wide variety of tops.
  • Skinny Jeans - Features a body contouring fit that tapers to a tight calf and small leg opening. This style fits slim and sleek through the ankles, creating a cute look that goes great with just about any type of shoes.
  • Straight-leg Jeans - These jeans are fitted through the hips and thighs. Because they create a straight visual line along the legs, they're perfect for wearing with a tailored striped shirt or blazer. For a more casual look, opt for straight jeans paired with a T-shirt and sandals or boots.
  • Curvy Jeans - This style typically best fits women with curvy hips by providing them room in the thighs with ample stretch to comfortably fit their shape. With extra room to accommodate their body, these jeans also enhance their curves.
